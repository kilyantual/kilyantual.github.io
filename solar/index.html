About our technology:

At Solar®, we pride ourselves on ensuring that our products are of the highest possible quality. It is with this spirit that we and our collaborators have developed the best solar panel ever developed. This is the Solar® System 3®, this solar panel requires as little solar energy as possible to operate, it is the most resistant solar panel on the market and its performance has never been observed with other manufacturers. We created it from sulphated silicon monoxide, a substance that absorbs almost all of the sun's rays. We will be happy to work with you to install the Solar® System 3® on your roof.
